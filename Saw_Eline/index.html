Intro:

Hello Ida. You don’t know us, but we know you.

I want to play a game. Here is what happens if your friends lose.

Do you see that bottle of shot over there?
The one by the TV? You must drink it. By youtself.

There is only one way to save you. Your friends must gather in 4 groups. Do it now. Quickly

Task 1:

Your first task is memory. I will give each group 30 seconds to memorize the pictures, before flipping them.

Your team must gather in a queue, and search one by one.

If you get your group one point, you can move back at the line. But if you lose … your team has lost a player. Happy hunting.

Congratulations to the winning team, you got the most points. As your reward I will let you eliminate one group. Choose wisely.

Task 2:

Your second task is simple. In front of you is a cube. The group who gets closest to solving it wins. The loser..? Well, you don't want to lose.

Congratulations to the winning team. But, I would not celebrate yet. The game has just started, and you are all in it. You may eliminate another team. Choose wisely. Now.

Task 3:

There are eight coloured tiles on the table. Your mission is simple; place the five coloured tiles at the correct place. You only get one try before you must get back into line. Good luck.

Congratulations, you have all the correct colors in all the right places. But do not relax just yet. So far. You have worked well as a group, but now you must fight alone.

Task 4:

Here is a riddle. The first person to guess the riddle is automatically going to the next challenge. There will be a new riddle after this.

What has to be broken before you can use it?

Congratulations, you won the first riddle.

What is always in front of you, but can't be seen?

Congratulations, you won the second riddle.

Now, answer me this. What goes up, but never comes down?

Congratulations, you won the third riddle.

Task 5:

As for the last and final task. We will play a new game.

On the floor in front of you. There are two decks of cards. You will compete against each other. The one who makes the tallest house of cards in tree minutes. Win the game.

Try not to fail. But there can only be one winner. The time starts now.

Congratulations. You have won the game. But tell me. Was it worth it? Was it worth eliminating your friends? Was winning this game worth it?

Final Challange

One last game awaits you. In Ida Regines guest bedroom there is a chocolate hidden, you have 1 minute. Happy hunting.
